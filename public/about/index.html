I am cybersecurity expert experienced in driving connected product security development and certification. I am also an active contributor to the standardization of product security at CEN-CENELEC, CSA and ITU.

Expertise

My professional career has spanned twenty-plus years working at Philips, NXP and currently Signify in the field of connected product development with strong focus on product security. My expertise includes:

  • Operational (OT) and Information (IT) technology product security including integration between OT and IT
  • Network access control: 802.1x, 802.11i
  • Wireless protocols: Zigbee, Matter, LiFi, WiFi, Bluetooth, NFC
  • Smart cards, secure elements and Trusted Platform Modules (TPM) based solutions
  • Trusted Execution Environment (TEE) based solutions
